Student Mental Health Support Platform

A comprehensive mood tracking application designed specifically for students to monitor their mental health and emotional well-being. This platform helps students track their daily moods, identify patterns, and maintain better mental health throughout their academic journey.

Key Features:

  • Daily mood logging with emoji-based interface
  • Mood history tracking and visualization
  • Firebase integration for data persistence
  • Clean, student-friendly user interface
  • Mental health pattern analysis
  • Privacy-focused design for sensitive data

Technologies Used:

  • HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript
  • Firebase (Backend & Database)
  • Responsive Web Design
  • Modern UI/UX Principles
